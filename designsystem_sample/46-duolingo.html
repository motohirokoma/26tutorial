Brand approximated

Duolingo

Duolingo's brand revolves around a friendly green owl mascot and a chunky, generous visual language designed to make language learning approachable and rewarding. Type runs heavy (700-900), shapes round (16-20px radius), and every interaction has a tactile, slightly bouncy quality that turns study sessions into a game.

Joyful

Bright primary green, playful illustrations, and surprise rewards keep practice sessions lighthearted.

Rewarding

Streaks, XP, gems, and chest animations celebrate every win, however small.

Universal

Built for 40+ languages, low literacy, and global cultures — UI relies on icons, color, and motion over copy.

Habit-forming

Daily goals, friend leagues, and gentle owl reminders nudge consistent practice.

Form approximated Official ↗

Duolingo forms favor large rounded inputs (16-20px radius), big tap targets (44px+ height), and obvious selection states. Errors borrow the Cardinal red from streak/heart loss patterns.

Feedback approximated Official ↗

Feedback in Duolingo is loud and animated. Correct answers get a satisfying green chime; wrong answers nudge with red and a friendly hint. The visual side is borrowed from card patterns: solid bottom border for tactility.
