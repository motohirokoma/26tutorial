Brand approximated

Lexicon

Lexicon is Liferay's design system for enterprise content management, portals, and digital experience platforms. Strong Liferay Blue anchors a structured grid feel suited to page-builder authoring, role-based admin, and large taxonomy navigation.

Structured

12-column grid and consistent 4px corners give CMS layouts predictable rhythm.

Open

MIT-licensed Lexicon plus Clay Icons keep extensions and themes friction-free.

Accessible

All blue/text pairs target WCAG AA; tokens flag inadequate contrast at build time.

Adaptable

Light, dark, and high-contrast themes ship from a single token source.

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Lexicon cards are 4px corners, 1px borders. Highlighted variants use a 2px Liferay Blue border; horizontal cards lay out icon + content side-by-side for inbox lists.
