Brand approximated

Canvas

Canvas is Workday's design system for enterprise HR, finance, and operations products. The system favors conservative 4px corners, calm blue tokens, and dense data-grid layouts that work at scale across payroll, HCM, and ledger surfaces.

Trustworthy

Conservative shapes and saturated blues signal reliability for finance-grade workflows.

Efficient

Dense tables, batch actions, and inline editing keep payroll and ledger work fast.

Inclusive

All token pairs target WCAG AA, with high-contrast variants for sensitive views.

Consistent

Shared tokens unify React, iOS, and Android surfaces across the Workday suite.

Color documented Official ↗

Canvas Blue is the dominant brand hue, supported by a calm Soap neutral ramp. The 9-step blue ramp spans 100 - 900 with 500 as the primary action color and 700 as the deep brand tone.

Typography documented Official ↗

Buttons documented Official ↗

Canvas defines five button variants: Primary (blue 500), Secondary (outline), Tertiary (text), Inverse (on dark surfaces), and Delete (destructive). Heights: 28 / 36 (default) / 44.

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Cards rest on body BG #F0F5F9. Default cards use a 1px border and gentle elev-1 shadow on hover. Modals and popups use elev-3.

Standard card

Border + hover shadow. Used for grouping form inputs and dashboard tiles.

Elevated card

No border, persistent elev-2 shadow. Floats above body in dashboard contexts.
