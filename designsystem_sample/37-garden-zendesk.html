Brand documented

Garden

Garden is Zendesk's design system for customer-experience products. Deep Kale teal carries support-product gravitas; Cactus, Lemon, Crimson, and Royal accents add warmth and clarity for ticketing, agent workspaces, and help-center surfaces.

Human

Warm tones and rounded corners keep support workflows feeling friendly under pressure.

Confident

Deep Kale anchors the system as a trusted, calm constant for agents.

Adaptive

Tokens scale across light, dark, and high-contrast for 24-hour support teams.

Open

Apache 2.0 React + tokens; published openly so partners can integrate without friction.

Color documented Official ↗

Garden organizes color into product hues (Kale, Cactus, Crimson, Lemon, Royal) plus a neutral Grey ramp. Kale-700 is the brand anchor; 500/600 carry interactive states.

Typography documented Official ↗

Garden uses Inter as its product typeface. The scale is Display / Heading / Body / Label, plus a mono variant for tokens and code.

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Garden surfaces (Tiles in their nomenclature) use 8px corners, 1px borders, and lift on hover. Selected tiles take on Kale-100 background with Kale-500 border.
