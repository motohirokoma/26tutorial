Brand approximated

B Backpack

Backpack is Skyscanner's open-source design system, built around a confident Skyscanner blue and friendly travel illustrations. The system supports web, iOS, and Android with shared tokens, and is tuned for multi-language travel UX across many global markets.

Honest

Show traveler-friendly information clearly — no hidden fees, no dark patterns. Trust is core to the brand.

Inviting

Soft rounding and the bright Skyscanner blue make the product feel welcoming across cultures.

Helpful

Components are tuned for international UI: long German strings, RTL Arabic, and dense East Asian text.

Color documented Tokens
