Brand approximated

S Stripe-style

Stripe's design language pairs editorial Söhne typography with a signature purple-to-cyan gradient, layered subtly across hero surfaces and CTAs. The palette is restrained, the typography is razor-sharp, and the overall mood is engineered yet human — financial infrastructure that feels approachable.

Editorial

Söhne-driven type system with deliberate weight and rhythm; copy is part of the product surface.

Iridescent

Purple-to-cyan gradients carry brand memory across hero, CTA, and visual divider treatments.

Engineered

Tight 4px grid, 6/8/12px radii, subtle shadows — every pixel placed with intent.
