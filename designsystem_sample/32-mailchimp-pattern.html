Brand documented

Mailchimp

Mailchimp's pattern library codifies the brand's quirky, illustrative voice. The combination of Cavendish Yellow and Peppercorn (near-black) reads instantly as Mailchimp, while a serif-italic display face anchors the playful yet professional tone.

Distinct

Cavendish yellow + Peppercorn = an unmistakable visual signature in the marketing-tech space.

Friendly

Quirky illustration, hand-drawn flourishes, and approachable copy build trust with small businesses.

Direct

Flat surfaces, hard borders, and offset block shadows give CTAs unmistakable affordance.

Typography documented Official

Buttons documented Official
