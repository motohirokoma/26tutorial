Branddocumented

Atlassian

The Atlassian Design System (ADS) provides tokens, components, and content guidelines used across Jira, Confluence, Trello, Bitbucket, and other Atlassian Cloud products. Core values: bold, optimistic, practical, with the recognizable B-blue.

Bold

Confident color, type, and motion that speak with clarity.

Optimistic

Encouraging tone — every interaction supports forward motion.

Practical

Token-first scaling across Jira, Confluence, and Trello surfaces.
