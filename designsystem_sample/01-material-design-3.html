Brand documented

Material 3

Material Design 3 (Material You) emphasizes personalization through dynamic color, expressive shape, and adaptive layouts. Tokens are derived from a seed color via HCT, allowing whole-app theming from a single source.

Personal

Dynamic color adapts to wallpaper, brand seed, or user choice.

Adaptive

Layouts respond fluidly across phone, foldable, tablet, and desktop.

Expressive

Variable type, motion, and shape carry meaning, not just polish.

Buttons documented Official ↗

M3 defines six button styles in increasing emphasis: Text, Outlined, Elevated, Tonal, Filled, plus Icon. Pill shape (50% radius) by default.

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Three card variants — Filled, Elevated, Outlined — plus tonal containers, modals, and popovers.

Filled card

Filled cards rest on a tonal surface for low-emphasis groupings.

Elevated card

Elevated cards use shadow + tonal overlay to step up emphasis.

Outlined card
