Brand documented

MUI

MUI (Material UI) is the most popular React component library implementing Material Design. It ships a comprehensive theming system based on createTheme(), supports emotion + styled-components, and includes 50+ components ranging from Button to Data Grid.

Composable

sx prop, styled() utility, and theme override layers in a clear inheritance chain.

Themed

createTheme() drives palette, typography, shape, breakpoints, and component overrides.

Accessible

WAI-ARIA practices baked into every primitive — keyboard, focus, and roles handled.

Typography documented Official ↗

Roboto is the default. Variants: h1-h6, subtitle1/2, body1/2, button, caption, overline. Default font weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 700 (bold).

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Card, Paper, and Dialog are MUI's primary surfaces. Paper has 24 elevation levels (0-24); Card defaults to elevation 1 and supports outlined variant.

Card · default

Elevation 1 — sits on the page with subtle shadow.
