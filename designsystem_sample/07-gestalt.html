Branddocumented

Gestalt

Gestalt is Pinterest's open-source design system, prioritizing accessibility, high-contrast Pinterest Red, and pin-forward visual rhythm. Tokens scale across web and mobile surfaces.

Accessible

WCAG-grounded color & focus, keyboard-first interactions across every component.

Image-forward

Pin and Masonry layouts emphasize visual content over chrome.

Bold contrast

Pinterest Red on white, strong type, generous radius for warmth.
