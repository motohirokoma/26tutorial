Brand documented

Vuetify

Vuetify is the most complete Vue UI component framework, providing 80+ Material Design-compliant components with rich theming, props-based variants, and accessibility built in. The default light theme uses Material Blue 700 (#1976D2) as primary.

Material

Faithful to Google's Material Design specification with token-driven theming.

Vue-native

Composition API, reactive props, and SSR-friendly rendering.

Themeable

CSS variables, dark mode, and per-component variants out of the box.
