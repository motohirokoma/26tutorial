Brand inspired

N Notion-inspired

Notion's product aesthetic centers on a paper-like canvas — warm off-white surfaces, generous whitespace, and an editorial serif heading paired with Inter body text. The signature is calm: minimal radii, restrained color, and content that breathes.

Editorial

Serif headlines + Inter body create a magazine-like reading rhythm.

Warm whitespace

Off-white #FAFAF8 surfaces feel paper-like rather than screen-glare clinical.

Sharp & flat

3–4px radii, no shadows by default. Hierarchy by spacing, not depth.

Color approximated Official ↗

Notion's palette is text-forward — neutrals carry 90% of the surface. Brand red #E03E3E appears sparingly as accent. The ten "tag" colors (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, pink, brown, gray, default) form the canvas's expressive palette.

Typography approximated Official ↗

Notion pairs a serif heading family with Inter body. Block-by-block hierarchy is structural — H1 / H2 / H3, then body, with quotes, callouts, and inline code as supporting blocks.
