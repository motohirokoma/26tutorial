Radix Themes

Radix Themes is a pre-styled component library from WorkOS, built on top of Radix Primitives. It pairs accessible behavior with the renowned 12-step Radix Color scale, scale and accent props for theming, and a tightly composed type and shape system. Source status is homepage-only — fidelity is intentionally marked approximated.

12-step color

Radix Colors are designed for every UI need: hover states, borders, accessible text, all in 12 perceptually balanced steps.

Theme prop

Wrap your app in <Theme> and switch accent (12 colors), gray scale, scale (1-3), radius, and panel background.

Variant system

Solid, soft, surface, outline, ghost, classic — each variant is consistent across Button, Badge, Card, IconButton.

Color documented Official ↗

Radix exposes 12-step scales for every accent. Each step has a defined role: 1-2 backgrounds, 3-5 ui surfaces, 6-8 borders/separators, 9 solid (the brand step), 10 hover, 11 accessible text, 12 high-contrast text.

Typography documented Official ↗

Radix Themes exposes a 9-step type scale (text 1-9), each with paired font-size, line-height, and letter-spacing tokens. Default font is Inter; the theme accepts a custom font family.

Buttons documented Official ↗

Surfaces documented Official ↗

Card variants — surface (default), classic, ghost. Theme panel toggles a translucent background. Modals (Dialog) compose Card-like surfaces with a backdrop and Escape-to-close behavior from Radix Primitives.

Surface card

Default panel surface. Renders as a 1px ring on top of the page background.

Classic card

Adds a subtle shadow for slight elevation without leaving the panel surface.

Tinted surface

Use the gray-2 background to nest a related grouping inside another panel.
