Brand inspired

Cal Cal.com

Cal.com is the open-source Calendly alternative. The brand uses a striking display weight (originally Cal Sans, here approximated with Inter 800), a near-black brand neutral, and indigo accent color. The product UI is light, soft-bordered, and visibly calmer than most SaaS dashboards.

Open

AGPL-3.0 source available. Self-host, fork, or use the hosted version.

Editorial

The display weight is the brand. Big, confident headlines anchor every marketing surface.

Calm UI

Soft borders, generous whitespace, neutral grays. Indigo appears only when needed.

Scheduling-first

Every primitive in the system supports a single core flow: pick a slot, book a meeting.

Color inspired Reference ↗

Cal.com's product palette is anchored by near-black #111827 as the brand neutral and indigo #6366F1 as the accent. The grayscale follows a warm-cool neutral mix in the Tailwind family.

Surfaces inspired Reference ↗

Cal.com surfaces are soft-bordered, generously padded, and rarely shadowed. The booking card is the brand's signature pattern — a two-pane layout with event meta on the left and time slots on the right.
