Branddocumented

L LINE Design

LINE Design System (LDSM) is published by LINE Corporation (now LY Corporation) to align the LINE app and family services. Identity centers on the iconic LINE Green and warm, rounded, approachable shapes optimized for messaging and casual interactions.

Closer

Bring people closer through familiar, friendly visual language.

Comfortable

Soft corners and generous spacing make daily use pleasant.

Easy

Predictable patterns lower the cognitive load for everyday actions.

Joyful

Stickers, icons, and color reinforce LINE's playful tone.

Primary typeface: Noto Sans JP (Google Fonts) with system fallback. Weights used: 400 / 500 / 700.
