Brand documented

U Untitled UI

Untitled UI codifies a visual language used by thousands of SaaS products: a calibrated grey scale, an unmistakable purple primary, and a 4-pixel base spacing rhythm. The kit favours generous breathing room over information density and ships every component as a fully-tokenised Figma library.

Time-saving

Hundreds of pre-built components, patterns, and pages ready to drop into a Figma file.

Tokenised

Every colour, type style, and shadow is a Figma variable / token, mappable to Tailwind or CSS.

Production-ready

All components include responsive variants, focus rings, hover, disabled, and dark themes.

Composable

Atomic primitives compose into pages; teams keep design + dev in lockstep.

Typography documented Official ↗

Inter is the default typeface. The display scale runs from 2xl (72/90) down to xs (24/32), with text sizes from xl down to xs. Headings sit at 600; body text at 400/500.

Buttons documented Official ↗

Untitled UI codifies five hierarchies (primary, secondary, secondary gray, tertiary, link / destructive) across six sizes (sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl). Focus rings use a 4px purple halo for primary actions.
