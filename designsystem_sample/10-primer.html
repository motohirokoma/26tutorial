Branddocumented

Primer

Primer is GitHub's design system, powering github.com and GitHub products. It centers on developer-tuned blue (#0969DA), the GitHub system font stack, and tight monospace integration for code-first interfaces.

Developer-first

Code, diffs, and terminals are first-class — typography and density reflect that.

Open source

All Primer packages and Octicons ship under MIT for community contribution.

Accessible

WCAG-grounded color and focus states, keyboard support across every component.
