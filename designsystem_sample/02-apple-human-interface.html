Human Interface

Apple's HIG codifies a unified visual language across iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, and visionOS. The system prioritizes content over chrome through clarity, deference, and depth — three pillars formalized in iOS 7 and refined through subsequent platforms.

Clarity

Legible text, precise icons, and crisp glyphs at every size; functional adornments only where they serve.

Deference

Fluid motion and translucent layers help content remain primary while UI recedes.

Depth

Distinct visual layers and realistic motion convey hierarchy and aid orientation.
