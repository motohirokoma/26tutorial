Brand approximated

Audi UI

Audi UI expresses the brand's premium automotive heritage in digital surfaces. Pure black backgrounds, sharp 0px-2px corners, and a single decisive red accent define a precision aesthetic — restrained chrome that lets product photography and content lead.

01 — Principle

Progressive

Forward-looking digital craft, mirroring the engineering ambition of the Audi product.

02 — Principle

Sophisticated

Restrained palette, precise typography, and angular geometry signal premium without ornament.

03 — Principle

Sportive

Tight motion, decisive red accent, and confident contrast convey performance.

04 — Principle

Authentic

Honest digital expression of Audi craft — no decorative effects, no false depth.

Buttons approximated Source

Sharp 0px corners, all-caps labels with 1.5px letter-spacing, and a 44px tap target. Primary action uses Audi Red; secondary uses pure black; outline ghost is reserved for tertiary moves.

Form approximated Source

Audi inputs use a clean underline pattern: no background fill, a single 1px gray bottom border, and a thicker red border on focus. Labels are uppercase caps with strong letter-spacing.
