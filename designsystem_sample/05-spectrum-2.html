Spectrum 2

Spectrum 2 is Adobe's evolution of its open design system, unifying Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, and Experience Cloud surfaces. The system emphasizes content-forward layouts, refined density, and an adaptive token model that scales across desktop, web, and mobile.

Adaptive

Density and scale tokens flex from desktop to mobile while preserving visual rhythm.

Expressive

A rich, signature palette led by Spectrum Blue, complemented by saturated accents.

Consistent

Token-driven foundations harmonize Adobe's product family while permitting brand voice.

Inclusive

WCAG-aligned contrast and tested motion patterns center accessibility from day one.

Buttons documented Official ↗

Spectrum 2 buttons: Accent (primary action), Primary (neutral high-emphasis), Secondary, Negative (destructive), Outline, Quiet. Pill-shaped corners. Sizes S (24h), M (32h, default), L (40h).
