Branddocumented

Polaris

Polaris is Shopify's open-source design system for building merchant experiences. It centers on the Shopify Green palette, generous 8px radius, and a warm, retail-friendly tone of voice.

Merchant-first

Every component supports a real merchant task — selling, fulfilling, growing.

Trustworthy

Clear hierarchy, calm density, and honest copy build merchant confidence.

Inclusive

Accessible color, type, and motion across global merchant geographies.
