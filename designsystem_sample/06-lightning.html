Branddocumented

SLDS

The Lightning Design System (SLDS) is Salesforce's open-source design language powering the Salesforce Platform — Lightning Experience, Mobile App, and Experience Cloud. Built for dense, data-heavy CRM workflows, SLDS leans on a Cloud-blue palette, Salesforce Sans, and conservative 4px corners.

Clarity

Eliminate ambiguity. Crisp affordances and consistent visual hierarchy.

Efficiency

Streamline and optimize workflows. Density without losing legibility.

Consistency

Create reusable patterns. One Salesforce, one experience.

Beauty

Demonstrate respect for the user's time and attention through quality.

Typography documented Official ↗

Salesforce Sans is the proprietary primary typeface. Reference uses Inter as a fallback within a system stack. SLDS leans on weight 300/400/700 with conservative line-heights tuned for dense data.
