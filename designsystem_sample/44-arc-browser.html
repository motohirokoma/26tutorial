Brand inspired

A Arc

Arc reimagines the browser around joy and intention. Its visual identity rests on three things: an instantly recognisable rainbow gradient, glassy translucent chrome, and a sidebar-first canvas that turns tabs into a living workspace.

Joyful

A vibrant rainbow gradient signals personality and play in a category dominated by grey chrome.

Glassy

Translucent surfaces with backdrop blur let the wallpaper colour breathe through every layer.

Sidebar-first

Tabs live in a vertical sidebar, freeing the viewport for content and giving each tab room to be itself.

Calm by default

Most chrome hides until needed. The browser disappears, the page takes the stage.

Motion & principles inspired

Arc favours soft, spring-driven transitions over linear easing. Tabs glide; menus pop; the rainbow gradient gently breathes on idle hero surfaces.

Design principles

Soft over sharp

Rounded corners, blur over solid borders, glass over flat fill.

Personal over uniform

Boosts let users restyle any site; Spaces let them tune their environment per context.

Calm by default

Chrome hides until hovered. The page wins, the browser steps back.

Joy is functional

Gradient and motion exist to make the everyday feel deliberate and delightful, not decorative.

Hover micro-interactions

Lift

Buttons translate up by 1px on hover and gain a softer glow shadow.

transform: translateY(-1px)

Glow

Primary CTAs cast a coloured halo that intensifies on hover, rooted in the magenta gradient stop.

box-shadow: 0 0 56px rgba(220,39,67,0.32)

Bloom

Pinned tabs gently scale on hover; Spaces switch with a wallpaper crossfade.

scale(1.02), 220ms standard ease

Signature features

Pinned tabs

The most-used tabs live at the top of the sidebar permanently — your daily desktop.

Today section

Tabs opened today auto-archive at midnight unless pinned, keeping the sidebar tidy.

Spaces

Switch entire workspaces — pins, themes, and tab sets — with a single shortcut.

Boosts

Restyle any site with custom CSS, fonts, or block selectors. Permanent visual tuning.

Little Arc

Pop-out window for one-shot browsing — peek at a link without committing it to a tab.

Arc Search

"Browse for me" — Arc summarises what you'd find by visiting top results, offered as a single page.

Sound & haptics

Arc ships with subtle audio cues — a soft "pop" on tab close, a celebratory chime on first launch. Sounds are optional and quiet by default.

Brand do / don't

Do

Reserve the rainbow gradient for the brand mark, hero, and primary CTA.

Do

Use blur and translucency liberally — chrome should always feel layered, not flat.

Don't

Apply the rainbow to body text or large surfaces — it loses meaning when oversaturated.

Don't

Use sharp 90° corners or hard borders — Arc is soft by definition.
