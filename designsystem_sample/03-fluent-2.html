Fluent 2

Fluent 2 is Microsoft's design language unifying Windows, Office, and Microsoft 365 across web, desktop, and native platforms. Built around a 4-pillar foundation, Fluent 2 embraces a refreshed brand ramp, refined typography (Segoe UI Variable), and consistent motion grammar.

Light

Layered surfaces, acrylic materials, and subtle elevation create perceived depth.

Depth

Layered z-space and shadow tokens bring order to dense product surfaces.

Motion

Predictable, accelerated easing aids spatial wayfinding without distraction.

Material

Acrylic and Mica backdrops give surfaces personality while supporting the system theme.
