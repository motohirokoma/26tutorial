Brand documented

Arco

Arco Design is ByteDance's open enterprise design system, with disciplined token names (text-1..4, bg-1..4, primary-1..9), a saturated brand blue, and dense layouts tuned for productivity tools.

Clear

Information hierarchy guided by precise token roles.

Consistent

Strict scale (1..9 for color, 1..7 for text/bg) keeps surfaces aligned.

Efficient

Component density and motion tuned for large-scale dashboards.

Beautiful

Restrained shadows, soft fills, and a saturated brand blue.
